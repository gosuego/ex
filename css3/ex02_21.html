Alice in Wonderland

When Alice follows the white Rabbit down a rabbit hole, she finds herself in a land where she can change her size. Shemeets a Duchess with a pig for a baby, a Cook that throws spoons, a wise but mad Chesheir cat, the March Hare, Mad Hatter and a Dormouse. A caterpillar shows her how to adjust her height by eathing from the right and left sides of a mushroom.
